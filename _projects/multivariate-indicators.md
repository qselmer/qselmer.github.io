## Scientific question

How can heterogeneous biological, fisheries, spatial, and environmental indicators be integrated into transparent and interpretable diagnostics of stock and ecosystem state?

## Scope

This project develops frameworks for combining indicators without obscuring their ecological meaning, temporal behaviour, uncertainty, or management relevance.

## Methods

- Indicator screening, redundancy analysis, and dimensional reduction.
- Clustering and selection of representative core indicators.
- Standardization, weighting, and multivariate index construction.
- Sensitivity, uncertainty, and robustness analysis.
- Change-point, regime, and threshold detection.
- Pressure–state interpretation and management-oriented visualization.

## Current status

Active methodological programme associated with the Multivariate Health Index of anchovy and broader pressure–state applications in marine and fisheries assessment.

## Expected outputs

- Multivariate indicator frameworks and diagnostic protocols.
- Scientific manuscripts and conference contributions.
- Reproducible code for indicator construction and evaluation.
- Visual products for communicating multidimensional stock condition.

## Relevance

The project seeks to preserve the information contained in individual indicators while producing integrated evidence that is auditable and useful for monitoring, assessment, and adaptive management.
