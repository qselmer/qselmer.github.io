## Overview

This paper develops classification models to infer gonadal maturity stages from the **gonadosomatic index (GSI)** in Peruvian anchovy (*Engraulis ringens*). The study addresses the need to harmonize historical reproductive observations with the maturity scale currently used in biological monitoring.

## Main contributions

- Operational classification of maturity stages from historical monitoring information.
- Evaluation of machine-learning classifiers for maturity-stage prediction.
- Harmonization of long reproductive time series under a consistent maturity framework.
- Support for reproducible assessment of reproductive indicators in fisheries monitoring.
