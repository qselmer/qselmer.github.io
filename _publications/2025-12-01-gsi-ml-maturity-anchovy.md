### Overview
This paper develops classification models to infer gonadal maturity stages using the **gonadosomatic index (GSI)**, with a case study on the **Peruvian anchovy (Engraulis ringens)**. The goal is to improve consistency across time by harmonizing historical biological records with the current maturity scale.

### Key contributions
- **Operational classification** of maturity stages from historical monitoring data.
- Evaluation of **machine-learning classifiers** for maturity-stage prediction.
- Practical pathway to standardize long time series of reproductive indicators for fisheries monitoring.
