## Blue Ecological Systems

Blue Ecological Systems is the applied collaboration and consulting component of my scientific work. It is intended to translate quantitative methods, ecological knowledge, and reproducible scientific computing into practical analyses, technical products, and decision-support tools.

Potential areas of collaboration include statistical analysis, fisheries and ecological modelling, stock-assessment support, spatio-temporal analysis, scientific visualization, reproducible workflows, methodological review, and technical training.

For academic collaboration or technical enquiries, please use the [contact page](/contact/).
