{% endif %} ## Record and metadata policy Each public record must have a verified title, authorship, year, output type, publication status, and stable source. When available, records also include DOI or repository identifiers, work URL, publication venue, citation, contributors, language, and related files. ORCID is retained as a source for discovery, reconciliation, persistent identifiers, and future synchronization; the curated website record remains the single public display record. The canonical category field for future records is documented in `_data/publication_taxonomy.yml`. Talks and posters are not publication categories and belong in the Conferences collection.
